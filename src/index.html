About

Founder of Team LTG, Martin Péril, established the company after 15 years working with SMEs in the hospitality, retail, cosmeceutical, real estate, finance and technology sectors.

After dealing with employees and company directors on a daily basis through statewide training initiatives, recruitment and human resource management, Martin noticed a common thread amongst each business. Employers/business owners were so consumed with revenue-generating activities and their never-ending list of 'things to do' to run their company, that they were becoming more and more disconnected with their people and culture.

Martin realised that in order for a business to sustainably thrive, there needed to be a focus and consistent approach to human resource management, development, performance management and recruitment that started with redefining the business’s culture.

Team LTG offers a tailored solution to human resource management for small to medium-sized businesses. When you work with Team LTG, we not only assess your workplace culture, staffing arrangement and contracts to ensure they comply with employment regulations, we provide REAL, practical solutions to enhance employee engagement, performance and communication.

Through personality profiling, values assessment, client-side recruitment, HR documentation and workplace training and development, we create peace of mind while reducing your load so you can focus on the bigger picture.
